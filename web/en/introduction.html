    When the game enters the genetic era, the game will provide male idols. Each idol will be given a variety of "DNA" codes. "DNA" will give characters such as height, weight, skin color, ochre, hairstyle, personality and other attributes. The combination of different "DNA" codes will affect the characteristics of the next generation of idol generation.
