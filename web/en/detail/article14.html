How to recharge and extract in game

First, how can I recharge GAS into the game?

1.Open the blackcat wallet, click Exchange in SGAS token then input the password and amounts.


1GAS=1SGAS


2. Click Exchange SGAS.


3. You can see Wallet SGAS in this page. Assets in the game are divided into Market Balance and Match Balance. Market Balance is for gladiator transaction and player can use it to buy gladiators and rent genes. The reward that players earn through market will also be saved here. Match Balance is for Cup contest. Player can use it to pay registration fee, and the reward that players earn through match will also be saved here. Market Balance and Match Balance cannot be used in common.


4. Click Charge-in, input amounts that need to be transferred, and then click Recharge for confirmation, then SGAS can be converted into the equivalent corresponding balance. You can use it directly in the game after you get Market Balance and Match Balance.


Second, how can I extract assets in the game into GAS?

1.When you want to extract assets in the game, click Charge-out button in Exchange SGAS page to extract Market Balance and Match Balance into SGAS. Withdrawal of Match Balance needs to be reviewed within 2 working days. If the withdrawal does not arrive in time, please be patient.


2.If you want to extract SGAS into GAS, you need to click Withdraw button in GAS token, input the wallet password and withdraw SGAS into the equivalent GAS. In this way, you can turn the game reward into a real digital asset.
