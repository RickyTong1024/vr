NeoGirl FAQ

  • What is "Neo.Girl"?

    "Neo.Girl" is a game that combines blockchain technology with the elements of developing games. Players can experience the innovation of the blockchain and the growth experience of the game. The technology of blockchain makes online transactions safe, reliable and secure. Virtual assets are value-for-money, easy to trace, and never disappear. Here, each player will belong to their own idol team and be able to interact and compete with each other. Players can experience the innovation of the blockchain and the fun of growing the game.

How to start

  • What do you need to do to play Neo.Girl?

    You only need a mobile phone with Internet access, and a digital wallet that supports and contains NEOGAS currency for your online transactions and withdrawal of your digital assets.

  • What is NEOGAS currency?

    NEOGAS is the operational token that powers the smart contracts and tokens built on the NEO blockchain (formerly known as AntShares) The maximum total cap is 100 million, and the minimum unit of GAS currency is 0.00000001.

  • How to get NEOGAS?

    You can exchange NEOGAS on large digital currency exchange platforms such as the Huobi.Pro and Binance.

About FISH

  • How to earn FISH coins?

    In the "Neo.Girl" game, you can let your idol girl work to get FISH. And in the late game, idol girls participate in the draft, get the corresponding FISH reward after getting the ranking; you can also get FISH by selling the ownership of your existing fashion.

About the game

  • How to get an idol girl?

    At the beginning of the game, players can get the first generation of virtual idol girl - Luna. After the game enters the "genetic era", each of the first generation girls will have a random set of "DNA" groups, which can be combined to generate second generation idols according to the "DNA" group.

  • What are the "DNA" characters?

    Players can "gene iteratively" their first-generation characters with other players' first-generation characters, and then generate second-generation characters based on the clothing attributes of the first-generation characters. The first-generation characters will not be destroyed. In the "gene iteration", the clothing of the first generation character determines the attributes of the second generation character, and there is a probability of a rare character.

  • What happens to rare characters?

    When a gladiator performs a cloning operation, the newly created gladiator has the possibility to inherit rare skills or weapons of source of the gene. It is also possible to mutate new rare skills and rare weapons.

  • What is the role of fashion?

    In-game fashion grades can be divided into white, blue, purple, and orange 4 grades. Each piece of fashion is randomly assigned to a number of random attributes, the higher the quality of the fashion, the higher the attribute. Wearing high-quality clothes can get a better score in the draft competition and get a high bonus.

  • How to get fashion?

    Players can purchase official fashion on the trade floor. After the game enters the "UGC era", players can make their own fashion, and the fashion after production will randomly generate each grade.

  • What is a draft game?

    In "Neo.Girl", players play different styles for their characters and make drafts. The draft competition is divided into a single-player contest and a multiplayer draft. The better the grade in the competition, the higher the score in the draft, and the winner will receive a high bonus.

  • How to sell fashion?

    Set " Sale Setting" on the "My Lockers" page, confirm sale after setting start price, end price, and price decay time. Over time, price will automatically transition from start price to end price. If sale is successful, system will charge a certain amount of processing fees.

  • What is an open community?

    We will add an open community platform to the game where players can devote their roles to communicate with other players. Within the community, players can customize community content, and characters can interact with 90% of objects in the community.
